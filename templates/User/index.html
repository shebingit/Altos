About Us

ALTOS Technologies is a leading Software and Web development company based in Kochi, Kerala. With the latest technology and framework, we continuously strive to improve ideas as a team of young, innovative thinkers. We are located in the IT hub of Kerala, Infopark Kochi, and have been helping businesses and individuals realize their full potential for years with our expertise in Software, Web development, Mobile application development, 3D modelling, Game development etc. We are also providing On-the-Job training to students with the knowledge we have gained over the past few years in order for them to build a great career under professionals of multinational companies.

Having theoretical knowledge is not enough to sustain a newcomer in the industry. It is essential for students to accomplish real-world experience before they are hired to work in their fields on real projects in a real environment.

A company internship from ALTOS provides students with valuable insight into the way a company or industry operate in real-time situations. Moreover, the supervision of working professionals from the MNC companies will allow students to work with mentors who help them improve their skills and monitor their performance. During an internship with our company, students learn more about a specific topic while also bridging the gap between employers' job requirements and academics. They are prepared for the challenges of a full-time position within any organization, providing more insight into the specific topic.

It has become a requirement for even entry-level jobs to have some industry experience in today's world. Employers can be convinced that a candidate is an asset to their company if they see they have made the best use of their time learning and building skills, as it allows them to cope with the new environment quickly and to establish connections with industry leaders and professionals without delay. Upon gaining appropriate experience, students can not only list the duties and tasks that they performed, but also describe them in a thoughtful, honest, and specific manner to interviewers.
